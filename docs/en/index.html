Costs

To cover for the costs of the festival we are asking a contribution of ‚Ç¨40 per day on the festival. All costs the are related to the festival are covered by this fee. Our goal of the festival is not to make money. But in the end we want to break even on the costs. In the contribution the following costs are taken into account:

  • Breakfast
  • Lunch
  • Coffee/ tea
  • Snacks (melonas/ manzanas)
  • Beersd/ Wines, plenty
  • Costs soundengineer/ equipmentcosts
  • Rental costs tents
  • Gas for cooking and feeding the electrical generators
  • Facility costs. Like natural soap (to not spoil the lake) and dishwashing liquid)
  • Construction costs (screws, etc)

We will inventorize which days you'll be visiting the festival. Based on this info we'll ask you to transfer your contribution before the festival.

Besides the costs the cover for the festival expenses we will make sure vistors can donate money to the artists.

Important: if you have the idea that 40 euro per day is too much for you budget (for example because you invested all your money in bitcoin in 2021). Please let us know so we will be able to arrange something with you :)

Food & Drinks

We will make sure there is enough wine, (local) beer, drinking water and different syrups for lemonades. In case you are a huge alcophile we want to ask you to bring some extra booze. We will buy enough beer and wine but we're not counting on the extremes. We also take care of breakfast, lunch and dinner on 5, 6, 7 august. Several vegetarian and vegan options will be available. In case of any other requirements, please let us know as soon as possible.

Foodwise, think of wood oven pizza's from Hans, fresh baguette with confiture and French cheese and salads with local herbs and veggies.

Staying the night

At the terrain there is more than enough space to camp with your tent, car or camper. Please let us know before coming what you will take with you to camp in, and only drive on the terrain with assistance of someone of our team.

There is 1 bio (compost) toilet and there are several spaces where we can pee in nature, as long as we do not leave any toilet paper. We will hang up bags for disposal of toilet paper.

There are no showers or running water, however you can swim and wash yourself in the lake. Washing here is only allowed with a special biodegradable soap that will not interfere with the flora and fauna. We will provide this soap for everyone and it is strictly forbidden to use any other product in the water.

Where

Where? At the lake in the valley of Omont, North of France. Omont is approximately a 4 hour drive from Utrecht, in the middle of The Netherlands. Omont can not be reached by train and public transport. That's why we want to make sure people who are coming by car, can take people who do not have a car or license. We created a sheet for this, so let the carpooling adventure begin!
